Item Affixes

A few things to note before looking at these tables:
Items with yellow text mean that the affix can spawn on both magic and rare items.
Items with light purple text mean that the affix can only spawn on magic items, not rares.
The number in parenthesis after the item describes the iLvl that the item needs to be to have a chance at rolling that affix.
For some items, certain affixes spawn at different values at different iLvls. For instance, the "Stout" (+3-12 defense) prefix can spawn on Grand Charms. However, if the Grand Charm is iLvl 1, this prefix can only provide +3-5 defense. Once the Grand Charm is iLvl 7, the prefix can then provide +6-9 defense. In cases like this where the affix's values can be split between iLvls, brackets will follow the iLvl stating what the stat's range is for that iLvl. i.e., Grand Charm(1)[3-5](7)[6-9](12)[10-12].
